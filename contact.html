Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll in any course by visiting our Courses page, selecting the course you're interested in, and clicking the "Enroll Now" button. You'll need to create an account if you don't have one already.

Do you offer certification?

Yes! All our comprehensive courses include a professional certificate upon successful completion. This certificate is recognized in the beauty industry.

What if I need help during a course?

Every course includes access to our private student community and instructor Q&A sessions. You can also contact our support team for technical assistance.

Can I access courses on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and optimized for all devices. You can learn on your smartphone, tablet, or computer.
